A N I M A T I O N


Animation is something I enjoy, seeing as I can bring my illustrations to life. I have worked a lot with storytelling, designing characters and creating storyboards for animations done using After Effects or JavaScript/Greensock. I have found SVG-animations to be one of my favorite things to spend my time doing.

Interactive teaser my group and I showed to a group of students as an exhibition. I did several of the illustrations as well as some work in After Effects.

SVG animations.

Click animations.

Simple animations shown by clicking different buttons, triggering a series of movements. Coded in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Interactive animation.

In this group project, my character P-Rex was used for an interactive animation coded in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I did all the illustrations and the storyboard.
