UX design


In this theme I learned more about the business aspect of multimedia design. The task I got together with my group was to create a fictional digital agency and digital service. We coded a prototype website for the agency and created an interactive prototype for the service in Illustrator and After Effects.

Through this task and the business model canvas, I learned about various aspects of what is needed to start your own business. In addition, I learned about the importance of groupwork in a professional environment in order to create value. One of the things my group and I struggeled with was the revenue stream, and how our digital agency would generate profit.

Digital agency

Work my group and I did for our digital agency, ReKreate. I worked on the reports and coded parts of the company website.

Digital service

Our digital service "montree" is budget/receipt-saving app created mainy for students and for a sustainable future. I worked on the prototype and the illustrations.
